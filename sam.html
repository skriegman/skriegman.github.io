Sam Kriegman is an incoming Assistant Professor of Computer Science, Chemical & Biological Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering at Northwestern University and director of the Xenobot Lab. His work draws inspiration from the origin and subsequent evolution of life, and applies the underlying mechanisms of self-organization and natural selection to the creation of novel autonomous machines. These machines can in some cases perform useful work, or they may be used as scientific tools to understand how animals evolve, grow, move, sense, and think.
