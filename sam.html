Sam Kriegman is an incoming Assistant Professor of Computer Science, Chemical & Biological Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering at Northwestern University and director of the Xenobot Lab. His work seeks explanations for the emergence of life's complexity, and applies this knowledge to the creation of new kinds of technological, biological, and biohybrid systems. These systems can in some cases perform useful work, or they may be used as scientific tools to understand how animals evolve, grow, move, sense, and think.
